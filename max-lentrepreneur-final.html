The founder story

"I built my expertise to serve the entrepreneur who refuses to settle — the accredited investor who demands results, not excuses."

Rising from entry-level positions through Big Four experience at KPMG, David founded Brickell CPA, Inc. (dba Max Financial) in 2006. His rare triple credential — CPA, Real Estate Broker, and Series 65 Investment Adviser — allows him to serve clients across the full financial spectrum.

He teaches entrepreneurship and finance at FIU, Miami Dade College, and Millennia Atlantic University, and is a published author of business guides and historical fiction exploring Cuba's Republican era (1902–1959).

His practice integrates biblical business principles and serves entrepreneurs, small business owners, foreign investors, and accredited investors across construction, real estate, healthcare, and logistics.
